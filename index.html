Jamie Nam

PhD Researcher in Information Technology · HEC Montréal

I am a PhD Researcher at HEC Montréal's IT program, passionate about integrating informatics, business, and user-centered design to improve healthcare accessibility by addressing technology adoption challenges.

Research Interests

Healthcare Technology Adoption

How patients and clinicians adopt — or resist — new digital tools, and what design choices shape that decision.

Human–AI Interaction

Decision support systems that augment, rather than replace, human judgment in high-stakes settings.

Digital Health Adherence

Wearables and connected devices, and the behavioral patterns that sustain (or end) their use.
